When distraction appears, many people default to self-attack: "I should be better."

The problem is mechanical. Shame increases stress, stress reduces executive bandwidth, and behavior slips further.

Acceptance is not resignation. It means seeing reality clearly, then choosing a workable next step.

Gentleness is not the opposite of progress. It is the condition that keeps progress alive.
